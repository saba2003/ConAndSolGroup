Consulting and Solutions Group LLC (CSG)

Who We Are

Consulting and Solutions Group LLC (CSG) is an appraisal company that provides its clients with complete and effective appraisal services considering the Georgian business environment. CSG offers real estate and business (company share) valuation according to international standards. This service will be provided by a team of certified professionals who have many years of experience in this field.

If you are interested, you can order an assessment of both immovable and movable property.

Following receipt of your order, an expert will contact you at the email address or phone number you provided to discuss the details.

Once the expert discusses the details, a price for the service will be determined, and the valuation process will start as soon as the money is deposited.
